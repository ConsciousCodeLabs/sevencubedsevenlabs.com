2. Our Zero-Trust Data Philosophy

Core Principle: The Patent #65 API operates on a zero-trust, stateless architecture. We process your encryption requests in-transit and immediately discard all data after returning the response. We have no ability to access, read, or recover your encrypted data.

2.1 What We DO NOT Store

  • Plaintext: Your original data is never stored
  • Ciphertext: Encrypted output is not retained
  • Encryption Keys: Keys are generated, returned to you, and discarded
  • Pathways: Your chosen encryption pathway is not logged
  • Request/Response Content: API payloads are not logged
